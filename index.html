Where to eat

Vinodiario

An amazing restaurant in Salamanca! Near the Plaza Mayor, Salamanca is amust visit place to taste delicious food. You can also taste the amazing wine and enjoy the views. Suitable for vegetarians and it has vegan options as well.

Plaza de los Basilios, 37001 Salamanca, Espanha
Learn more

Mencia Restaurante

Also in Plaza Mayor Salamanca, this restaurant offers a service with private meals. Ideal if you want to celebrate a special occasion with friends or with your lover. It offers a wide variety of regional plates. It won travellers' Choice award in 2022.

C. Grillo, 11, 37001 Salamanca, Espanha
Learn more

Restaurante Isidro

Restaurante Isidro offers a wide variety of seafood, which is the specialty in this restaurant. It is another restaurant near Plaza Mayor Salamanca, where you can enjoy a relaxed meal with friends or family. It has vegan options as well. It's one of the best restaurants in Salamanca.

C. Pozo Amarillo, 37001 Salamanca, Espanha
